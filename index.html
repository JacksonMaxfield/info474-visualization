Which Antibiotic Has The Highest MIC Scores:

Coming into this data I didn't have any real idea as to what it might indicate. What do the MIC scores mean and how much do they vary across the different antibiotics. So my first specific question was:

Which antibiotic has the highest average scores across the various bacterial infections?

This is a decent first question to ask. It allows the viewer to quickly understand that Penicilin is multiple times better than Streptomycin or Neomycin for many of the bacterial infections.
